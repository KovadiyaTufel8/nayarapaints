Premium Wall Putty

Nayara Paints Premium Wall Putty is a white cement-based polymer modified putty. It provides a protective base for your expensive paints, ensuring a silky smooth finish and extended life for the topcoat.

Where to Use

  • Interior Masonry Walls
  • Exterior Plaster Surfaces
  • Renovation of Old Walls

Why Choose This Product?

  • Super Smooth: Ensures a glass-like finish for paint.
  • Strong Adhesion: Bonds strongly to the base plaster.
  • Water Resistant: Reduces potential for dampness.
  • Breathable: Allows trapped moisture to escape.
